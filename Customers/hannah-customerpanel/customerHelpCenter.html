FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

a. Is iCargo Pacific a registered company?
ㅤㅤYes, iCargo Pacific is a software supply chain solution company that started in 2019 in the Philippines.

b. To which destinations am I able to ship with iCargo Pacific's logistics partners?
ㅤㅤiCargo Pacific has an extensive network that supports delivery services intercity, inter-province, and across the island.

c. What are your office hours?
ㅤㅤOur company operates daily, including weekends and holidays. Office hours are from 8:00am to 5:00pm. Our hotline is available 24/7 at (+63) 917-692-4203 for any inquiries or concerns.

a. Where can I place my bookings for pick-up?
ㅤㅤYou can send your bookings through our website and mobile application. Customers are highly recommended to book their orders through our app and proceed to the nearest chosen logistics branch for drop-off or schedule pickup.

b. What are the list of charges for the services I use?
ㅤㅤList of charges are the Shipping Cost, Value-Added Service Fee, Cash on Delivery (COD) Fee, Value Added Tax (VAT) and other charges (if any).

c. What payment methods are supported by iCargo Pacific logistics partners?
ㅤㅤMode of payments at present are cash transactions such as Cash on Delivery (COD) and cashless transactions, depending on your chosen courier.

a. How can I track the status of my package?
ㅤㅤYou can track your packages by using our service platforms: website, mobile app and 24/7 hotline. Monitor your package in real time via these platforms.

a. Should I package my shipment?
ㅤㅤYes, but if package needs enhancements or improvements, our courier will advise.

b. What is the best way to package my shipment?
ㅤㅤAll items should be packed carefully before the rider arrives to pick up your shipment. Materials such as bubble wrap, styrofoam or styroflakes help to make sure that your package is shock-resistant. Customers may also avail of their chosen courier's packaging to be paid separately from the shipping fee.

a. Are there any limitations on the goods that I can send regarding weight and dimensions?
ㅤㅤDepending on your chosen logistics, the general accepted weight is up to 50 kilograms maximum on the weight of the package. For express shipments, items with length of more than 1.5 meters on one side will not be accepted.

b. Are there restrictions on the goods that I can send via J&T Express?
ㅤㅤYes, Here is a list of restricted items for shipping:

ㅤㅤㅤㅤa.) Dangerous goods & biological reagents such detonators, gunpowder, firecrackers, gasoline, alcohol, kerosene, Tung oil, lacquer, matches and pesticides;
ㅤㅤㅤㅤb.) Narcotic drugs and psychotropic substances, such as opium, morphine, cocaine, etc.
ㅤㅤㅤㅤc.) Items prohibited by circulation or delivery by national laws, such as arms, tobacco, currency, antiques, gold and silver jewelry, precious metals;
ㅤㅤㅤㅤd.) Perishable items such as fresh meat and fresh fish;
ㅤㅤㅤㅤe.) Various reactionary newspapers and magazines, publicity materials and obscene or indecent articles;
ㅤㅤㅤㅤf.) Specimens of various living animals, liquids and various animal corpses;
ㅤㅤㅤㅤg.) Other items that are prohibited by law and inappropriate packaging materials.

c. Which measurements do I need to provide for my package?
ㅤㅤKilograms (kg) in weight & centimeters (cm) in dimensions. Weight Calculations are based on the actual weight of the package versus the volumetric weight, accounted whichever is higher.

d. What is volumetric weight?
ㅤㅤVolumetric weight, sometimes also called "dimensional weight," is a billing technique which takes into account the length, width and height of a package.
ㅤㅤㅤㅤa.) Length: measure first left to right;
ㅤㅤㅤㅤb.) Width: measure front to back;
ㅤㅤㅤㅤc.) Height: measure top to bottom.

e. Should I send fragile items?
ㅤㅤYes, but our Courier will advise if it needs enhancements on packaging as we label all our fragile item shipments for proper handling.

f. How do I label my package?
ㅤㅤWe will provide an Air Waybill (AWB) or Electronic Waybill (E-waybill). Please make sure to provide all details correctly. Errors or mistakes can cause delays.

a. Do you offer insurance?
ㅤㅤFor the safety of your parcels, iCargo Pacific provides a value-added service, which is a special service for express mail. The value-added fee is 1% of the declared price of the item (according to the invoice of the item). e.g.If item price is P1,000.00, then the value added fee is P1,000.00 x 1% = P10.

b. In which situations can I file for a claim?
ㅤㅤIf a shipment is received in good condition, it is not possible to submit a claim. In the case of loss or damaged shipment delivery, it is essential that you mention the damaged state of the package on the manifest when receiving the goods. Also: take photos for proof.

c. How can I submit a claim for loss and damage?
ㅤㅤAll claims must be sent by mail through your respective chosen courier or through info@icargopacific.com. You can also file a report through our 24/7 hotline (+63) 0917-692-4203.

d. Within what time period should a claim for delayed, loss or damage be filed?
ㅤㅤClaims are valid depending on the following accepted conditions:

ㅤㅤㅤㅤ1.) Loss complaint
ㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤTime Limit: Within the time limit of the request (within 30 days from the date of the shipment).
ㅤㅤㅤㅤ1.) Damage complaint
ㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤTime Limit: Within the time limit of the request (scheduled within 7 days from thedate of notification of the damage).
